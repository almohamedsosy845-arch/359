About IFT-D

Company Profile

IFT-D is a leading financial information consulting firm based in Manchester, England. Founded in 2010, we have grown from a regional financial advisory firm to a global player with clients in over 30 countries across Europe, Asia, North America, and Australia.


We adhere to the service concept of integrity, professionalism and efficiency, integrate advanced data analysis technology and rich practical experience, and build a complete financial service system. Our team consists of senior financial analysts, industry experts and professional consultants who are familiar with global financial rules and market dynamics.


Over the past 14 years, we have served more than 2,000 corporate clients (including 50+ Fortune 500 companies) and 10,000+ high-net-worth individuals, helping them achieve an average annual return on investment that is 15% higher than the industry benchmark.
